Jog For Half An Hour A Day

Jogging is something which will target your overall body weight but it is obvious that you won't be able to do it for half an hour continuously, so there is a specific rule to keep your body in motion the whole time while jogging/running i.e. without sitting and giving your body a rest in middle of a jog, to make most out of it, I will call it as 10/2 Rule in which you have to jog for the maximum time you can and then once you have totally fatigued yourself, you have to slow down and start brisk walking till it feels like you can start the jog again.

Here, 10 denotes a 10 minute run/jog and 2 denotes a 2 minutes brisk walk which you have to do alternately. You can do the jog and walk for whatever time you want, it doesn't necessarily has to be 10 and 2 minutes respectively.

As being a beginner, now you know how can you start your physical training and after this we will be looking about the diet which is, you can say, more important than exercising. The reason why I am talking such is, you can loose weight by doing a little exercise and concentrating totally on your diet but you can not reach the top with just exercise and not focusing on the diet. Running For Half An Hour A Day.

As we have already discussed about the way you have to run/jog in Begnniers' section, i.e. by 10/2 Rule which is running till you don't fatigue yourself completely and then slowding down to start a brisk walk, now we will be going deeper into this section. If you decide to do a 30 minute run daily, set a timer of 15 minutes in your cell phone for first few days. As soon as the timer rings you, have to start running to your home back, by doing this you'll be able to run for perfect 30 minutes and on the 30th minute, you will reach your home.

Runing/jogging on the same route daily will avoid wastage of time in deciding the routes everyday. Once you get habitual of your route and time, try to avoid bringing your smartphones while your runs. In a day, at least 30 minutes you shall give for your body without any electronic distraction and I really don't think that there may be a need of your smartphones while running.
